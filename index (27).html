Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025. UniLoop is committed to protecting the privacy of every student and business that uses our platform.

What we collect

When a business submits a listing, we collect their business name, category, contact details, address, description, halal status, and an optional photo. When a student joins our waitlist, we collect their email address only. We do not collect any data from students who browse the platform without signing up.

How we use it

Business information is used solely to create and display listings on UniLoop. Student email addresses are used only to notify them when new listings go live in their area. We do not use any collected data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the platform.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with any third party. We use Formspree to process form submissions and Google Sheets to store listing data. Both are used solely as operational tools and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Your rights

Any business or individual whose information is stored on UniLoop may request that it be corrected or removed at any time by contacting us at nextlevelpicks99@gmail.com. We will action all requests within 48 hours.

Data security

All data submitted through UniLoop is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold and review our practices regularly.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please reach us at nextlevelpicks99@gmail.com. We take every inquiry seriously and respond promptly.
