Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025. UniLoop is committed to protecting the privacy of every student and business that uses our platform.

What we collect

When a business submits a listing, we collect their business name, category, contact details, address, description, halal status, and an optional photo. When a student joins our waitlist, we collect their email address only.

How we use it

Business information is used solely to create and display listings on UniLoop. Student emails are used only to notify them when new listings go live. We do not use data for advertising or profiling.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with any third party. We use Formspree and Google Sheets as operational tools only.

Your rights

Any business or individual may request correction or removal of their data at nextlevelpicks99@gmail.com. We action all requests within 48 hours.

Contact

Terms of Use

Last updated: March 2025. By using UniLoop, you agree to the following terms.

1. What UniLoop is

UniLoop is an English-language directory connecting international students in Tbilisi with local businesses. We provide a discovery service only — we are not a booking platform or party to any transaction.

2. For students

UniLoop is free to use. Listings are for informational purposes. Confirm details directly with the business before visiting.

3. For businesses

Businesses confirm submitted information is accurate. UniLoop may decline or remove listings with false or misleading content. Paid subscriptions are monthly with no penalty for cancellation.

4. Halal declarations

Halal status is self-declared by the business. UniLoop does not independently verify. Students should confirm directly with the business.
